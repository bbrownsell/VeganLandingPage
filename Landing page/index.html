The benefits of a plant based diet

Eating plants can save you and the planet.

Information about going plant based

  • One of the most powerful steps you can take to improve your health, boost energy levels, and prevent chronic diseases is to move to a plant-based diet
  • Recent science has shown changing your nutrition is a powerful way to live longer, help the environment, and reduce your risk of getting sick.
  • Scientific research highlighted in the landmark book The China Study shows that a plant-based diet can reduce the risk of type 2 diabetes, heart disease, certain types of cancer, and other major illnesses.
  • A whole-foods, plant-based diet lets you meet your nutritional needs by only eating natural, minimally-processed foods where none of the ingredients come from animals.
  • Examples of the foods you’ll enjoy on a plant-based diet include fruits, vegetables, tubers, whole grains and legumes.
  • There are plenty of other foods you can also enjoy— including nuts, seeds, tofu, tempeh, whole-grain flour and breads, and plant-based milks.
